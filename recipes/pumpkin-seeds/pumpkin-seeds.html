Pumpkin seeds

Description

Don't thow out your pumpkin seeds, use this quck and easy recipe to turn them into a delicious and healthy snack!

Ingredients

  • 1-1/2 cups raw whole pumpkin seeds (or however much you have saved but this ratio works best)
  • 2 teaspoons butter, melted
  • 1 pich of salt (consider substituting with your favorite seasonings)

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 300 degrees
  2. Toss seeds in a bowl with melted butter and salt
  3. Spread the seeds in a single layer on a foil lined baking sheet
  4. Bake for 45 minute or unti golden brown, stiring occasionally
