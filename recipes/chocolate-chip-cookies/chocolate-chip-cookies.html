Chcoclate chip cookies

Description

Enjoy this rich old fasion sugar cooke recipe from my family to yours

Ingredients

  • 1 cup + 2 tbsp flour
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 1/2 cup shortening
  • 1/2 bag of choclate chips

Directions

  1. In a large bowl mix the white sugar, brown sugar, egg, vanilla, and shortening
  2. In a second bown mix the flower, baking soda, and salt
  3. Adding small amounts at a time, blend the flower mixture into the large mixing bowl
  4. Once the two bowls are fully mixed stir in the choclate chips
  5. Bake at 375 for 10-12 minutes
