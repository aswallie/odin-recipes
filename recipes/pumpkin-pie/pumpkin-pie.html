Ingredients

  • 2 cups pumpkin
  • 1(12 ounce) can evaporated milk
  • 3 eggs
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1-1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1/2 teaspoon nutmeg, if desired
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cloves, if desired
  • 1 (9 inch) deep dish pie shell, or 2 shallow dish pie shells

Directions

  1. In a bowl mix the sugar, salt, spices, and eggs.
  2. Carefully add and mix pumpkin and evaporated milk
  3. Place on a baking pan and bake at 350 for 50-60 munites
  4. Insert toothpick in center of pie. If it comes out clean the pie is done
